Beef Biryani

Price: 300 /- 250/-

Beef Biryani is a hearty and flavorful dish made with basmati rice, tender beef, and a blend of aromatic spices. It is a popular choice among meat lovers and is often served during special occasions and celebrations.

The preparation of Beef Biryani involves marinating the beef in a mixture of yogurt and spices, which helps to tenderize the meat and infuse it with flavor. The rice is cooked separately with whole spices like cardamom, cloves, and cinnamon, giving it a fragrant aroma. The marinated beef is then layered with the cooked rice and slow-cooked together, allowing the flavors to meld beautifully.
