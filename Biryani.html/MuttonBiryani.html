Mutton Biryani

Price: 350 /- 300/-

Mutton Biryani is a rich and aromatic dish made with basmati rice, tender mutton, and a blend of flavorful spices. It is a favorite among meat lovers and is often served during special occasions and celebrations.

The preparation of Mutton Biryani involves marinating the mutton in a mixture of yogurt an d spices, which helps to tenderize the meat and infuse it with flavor. The rice is cooked separately with whole spices like cardamom, cloves, and cinnamon, giving it a fragrant aroma. The marinated mutton is then layered with the cooked rice and slow-cooked together, allowing the flavors to meld beautifully.
