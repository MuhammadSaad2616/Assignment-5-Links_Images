Chicken Biryani is a delicious and aromatic dish made with basmati rice, tender chicken, and a blend of flavorful spices. It is one of the most popular and beloved dishes in Pakistan and India, enjoyed on every special occasion and festive gathering.

The preparation of Chicken Biryani involves marinating the chicken in a mixture of yogurt and spices, which helps to tenderize the meat and infuse it with flavor. The rice is cooked separately with whole spices like cardamom, cloves, and cinnamon, giving it a fragrant aroma. The marinated chicken is then layered with the cooked rice and slow-cooked together, allowing the flavors to meld beautifully.
