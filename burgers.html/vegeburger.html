Veggie Burger

Price: 450 /- 400/-

A veggie burger is a delicious and satisfying alternative to traditional meat burgers. Made from a variety of plant-based ingredients such as beans, lentils, vegetables, and grains, veggie burgers offer a flavorful and nutritious option for those seeking a meat-free meal. They can be seasoned with herbs and spices to enhance their taste and are often served with fresh toppings like lettuce, tomato, onions, and avocado. Whether you're a vegetarian or simply looking to reduce your meat consumption, a veggie burger is a tasty choice that can be enjoyed by everyone.
